Zoilevium Topical

Zoilevium Topical is a mineral-based antimicrobial and disinfectant compound that has the properties and modes of action of a broad-spectrum, inorganic, metallo-antibiotic.

It has been known for a long time that various metals are effective in killing microbes and have historically been used for antibacterial purposes. An advantage to the use of metal-based metallo-antibiotics and disinfectants is their diverse modes of action.

The modes of action Zoilevium Topical spray relies on to assist with disinfection and anti-microbial efficacy, are found in the inherently acknowledged properties of the trace minerals it comprises, like:

Zinc: Has antibacterial properties, helps protect cells from oxidative stress, assists in wound healing. (A deficiency in zinc may cause delayed wound healing.)

Magnesium: Has anti-inflammatory properties, contributes to tissue formation, has a role in the process of cell division, contributes to wound healing.

Copper: Has anti-inflammatory properties, promotes the formation of red blood cells, has antibacterial properties, protects cells from damage, contributes to the protection of cells from oxidative stress, contributes to the production, maintenance, and repair of normal connective tissue, promotes wound healing.

All of these properties serve to promote the disinfection, and healing of open scrapes and wounds.

What makes us unique?

What gives Zoilevium Topical its beneficial synergistic qualities, are the unique mineral combinations, formulations and production processes, individually tailored for this product.

Indications:

Zoilevium Topical is a unique combination of above-mentioned minerals with their respective anti-microbial and tissue healing advantages, which may assist in topical wound disinfection and healing.

Contra-indications:

Zoilevium Topical is contra-indicated in persons who are hypersensitive or allergic to any of the ingredients.

Warning:

Statements made, have not been evaluated by the US Food and Drug Administration or any medical control council. This product does not claim to diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any disease.

Directions for use:

Spray undiluted product 3 times daily on affected area, and let dry. Do not wash off, except in case of severe burning sensation. May be covered by bandage. Continue regimen until healed.

We recommend that you see your health practitioner first in the following circumstances:

  • If the wound is very deep and causes major bleeding.
  • If you develop a high fever due to the infection of the wound.
  • If foreign objects are embedded.
  • In case of an animal or human bite.
  • If you have any uncertainty or questions
