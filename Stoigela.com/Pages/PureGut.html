Zoilevium PureGut

Zoilevium PureGut is a mineral-based, inorganic, antimicrobial compound. The modes of action it relies on to assist with gut-related problems and conditions like Crohn’s, IBD and IBS, are found in the inherently acknowledged properties of the trace minerals it comprises, like:

  • Copper: It has anti-inflammatory properties, promotes the formation of red blood cells, helps with the absorption of iron in the body, it has strong antibacterial properties, it protects cells from damage and helps to produce and repair connective tissue.

  • Zinc: Supports the body’s immune function, it promotes digestive health, it has antibacterial properties, it helps to normalise macro-nutrient metabolism, it supports connective tissue formation, it also helps the body to metabolise carbohydrates, fats and proteins.

  • Magnesium: It has anti-inflammatory properties, it helps metabolise carbohydrates, fats and proteins and contributes to tissue formation.

​​ All of these properties may contribute to counteracting the common symptoms of an irritated gastrointestinal tract.

Indications:

Zoilevium Puregut is a combination of above-mentioned minerals with their respective advantages, which may assist in alleviating the symptoms of Crohn’s Disease, IBD and other IBS related conditions, as the properties listed above speak directly to the symptoms of these ailments.

Contra-indications:

Zoilevium Puregut is contra-indicated in persons who are hypersensitive or allergic to any of the ingredients.

Warning:

If you are taking other medication/-s for Crohn’s and IBS, we do not advise that you stop the medication. Zoilevium Puregut is meant to be used as supportive therapy. Not recommended if pregnant and/or breast-feeding.

Statements made, have not been evaluated by the US Food and Drug Administration or any medical control council. This product does not claim to diagnose, treat, cure or prevent any disease.

Directions for use:

Adults: take 15 drops daily in a glass of water, or any other preferred safe, consumable drink. Do not drink undiluted. Dosage may be consumed in one sitting, or may be divided into any amount preferred, as long as 15 drops per day are consumed. Shake bottle gently before use. Always consult your health care practitioner, especially if you have underlying conditions and/or take other supplements or medication.
